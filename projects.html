✦Signature work

Three projects that define LJ Design.

Every project teaches us something — but a few change how we work. These are the three we are most proud of: press the + on any card to read its story.

Executive Lounge

A commercial lounge conceived as a calm horizon: curved cream sofas float on a hand-drawn rug, framed by floor-to-ceiling glazing and a ribbon of concealed light along the ceiling. Every sightline was composed so the room feels serene from any seat.

LocationSaudi Arabia
TypeCommercial
ScopeDesign, FF&E, fit-out & styling

The Mirror Room

Antique mirror panels wrap the salon, doubling a curated gallery wall and softening the light to a candle-glow. A cognac leather Chesterfield anchors the room — dark glamour layered with timeless character, made for long evenings.

LocationRiyadh
TypeResidential
ScopeDesign, bespoke joinery & styling

Moody Salon

Deep teal walls, a camel curved sofa and a wall of collected art — a living room with the atmosphere of a private members' club. Layered lamplight and rich textiles turn the room warm the moment the sun goes down.

LocationJeddah
TypeResidential
ScopeDesign, FF&E & home styling
