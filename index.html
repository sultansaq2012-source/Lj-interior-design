About

Meet Lubna

Lubna is a passionate interior designer dedicated to creating sophisticated and functional spaces that reflect each client's unique vision and lifestyle.

With over a decade of experience transforming residential and commercial interiors across Saudi Arabia, Lubna brings an extraordinary eye for detail, a mastery of proportion and light, and a deep understanding of how people truly live. From concept to execution, every project is approached as a personal collaboration — a journey to discover the essence of a space and elevate it into something truly remarkable.
