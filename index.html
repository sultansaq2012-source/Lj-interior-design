04Process

From first meeting to key in hand.

Every project follows a clear, calm path — so you always know what happens next, and who is taking care of it.

  1. 01

    Listen

    A long first conversation — about how you live, what you love, and what the space needs to become. No drawings yet.

    Consultation
  2. 02

    Concept & Moodboard

    Materials, palettes and atmospheres. We shape the direction together before a single wall moves.

    2–4 weeks
  3. 03

    Design & Documentation

    Detailed drawings, FF&E schedules and joinery packages — every junction resolved on paper before site.

    4–8 weeks
  4. 04

    Fit-Out & Build

    We manage contractors, suppliers and site visits — quality checked at every stage, so you only ever call one number.

    2–8 months
  5. 05

    Styling & Handover

    The finishing layer — styling, accessories and a walkthrough of your completed space, ready to live in.

    Final week
